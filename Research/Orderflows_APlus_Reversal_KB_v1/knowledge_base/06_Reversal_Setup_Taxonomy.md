# Reversal Setup Taxonomy

How his setups relate. Full per-setup detail is in `01_...Qualitative_Framework.md` §3; this is the map. Supports KPI 1.

## The single engine
All his reversals are one engine — **aggressor exhaustion / absorption at a location, confirmed by follow-through** — expressed through different *evidence*:

```
                 AGGRESSOR EXHAUSTION / ABSORPTION AT A SWING EXTREME
                                     │
        ┌──────────────┬────────────┼─────────────┬───────────────┐
   EXHAUSTION      ABSORPTION     IMBALANCE      DELTA          PRICE/LEVEL
   evidence        evidence       evidence       evidence        evidence
   ─────────       ──────────     ──────────     ─────────       ───────────
 • exhaustion    • value-area    • stacked      • ratio+div     • POC-on-extreme
   print (≤N)      absorption(A+)  (≥3, opp.col) • delta diverg  • prominent POC
 • single print  • stopping vol  • multiple     • cum-delta div • abandoned VA/POC
                 • iceberg/      • inverse/     • delta surge   • failed breakout
                   refreshing      trapped      • delta weakness• green delta trap
                                                • strong-delta
                                     │
                          CONFLUENCE (stack ≥2 dimensions) ──► higher tier
                                     │
                       CONFIRM: next 1–2 bars break signal bar  ──► TRADE
```

## Families
- **Exhaustion family** (his flagship): exhaustion print, single print, Exhaustion Entry Model. Evidence = *absence* of volume/aggression at the extreme.
- **Absorption family:** value-area absorption (A+), stopping volume, inverse/trapped imbalance, iceberg/refreshing. Evidence = passive liquidity eating aggression (counter-delta with no price move).
- **Imbalance family:** stacked, multiple, inverse. Evidence = diagonal bid×ask ≥4:1 clusters; color & location decide reversal vs continuation.
- **Delta family:** ratio+divergence, delta/CVD divergence, delta surge/weakness, strong-delta, Green Delta Trap. Evidence = delta vs price disagreement or decay.
- **Price/level family:** POC-on-extreme, prominent POC, abandoned VA/POC, failed breakout / holding-vs-failing. Evidence = where the level is and whether it's defended/abandoned.

## How they combine (the A+ recipe)
His best trades **stack families** at one location: e.g. *exhaustion print* (exhaustion) **+** *abandoned value area / prominent POC* (absorption+level) **+** *weakening then flipping delta* (delta) **+** *opposite-color stacked imbalance* (imbalance), all at a *clean swing extreme*, confirmed by an immediate break. That is the literal "A+." (Ch016/v482, v485, v490)

## Continuation vs reversal (do not confuse)
The same evidence can mean continuation: **same-color** stacked imbalances, **3 failed divergences** (trend day), delta surge in trend, value *gaps* (price leading value). Direction is set by **color + location + regime**, not the pattern alone.
