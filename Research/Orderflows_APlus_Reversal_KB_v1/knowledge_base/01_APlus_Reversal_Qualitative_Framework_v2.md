## 0. The one-paragraph model (unchanged)
A reversal = "the last buyer has bought / the last seller has sold." Aggressors exhaust at an extreme, aggression goes silent, price reverts. **React, don't predict; "not a red-light/green-light system."** A+ = **location + confluence + immediate follow-through + small-bar/tight stop.** (Ch017/v492; Ch016/v485; re-confirmed across the full corpus.)

## 1. Grading / tiering (unchanged ladder)
A+/my-favorite/textbook/perfect/French-kiss/gold-signal → great/beautiful/bread-and-butter → nice/decent → marginal/half-hearted/borderline → skip/"wouldn't qualify"/"kiss of death" → failed. Software tiers: Liquidity Finder 70–100/34–69/1–33; Algo 1(strong)/3/5; Imbalancer 1–6. `[v2]` Re-confirmed: **"more signals is not better"** — higher tool-magnitude = fewer, higher-quality signals (Ch082/v257). Adopt repo `Reversal_Hunter` A+/A/B/C scale.

## 2. Cross-cutting A+ vs mediocre discriminators
1. **LOCATION** (dominant) — at/within ~1 tick of a clean swing extreme/HOD/LOD after a real move. `[REPEATED]`
2. **CONFLUENCE** — "single signals lie"; but a screen, not arithmetic. `[REPEATED]`
3. **FOLLOW-THROUGH** — next 1–2 bars break the signal bar. `[v2]` **Timeframe-conditional** (off ≥5-min / ultra bonds); culls ~25–45% of signals — the load-bearing filter.
4. **SPEED** — works immediately; sideways = failing.
5. **CANDLE-COLOR** agreement (green bull / red bear).
6. **DELTA correct** — `[v2]` read **intrabar Max/Min vs close**: close-delta alone is misleading; a close near 0 having hit a large intrabar extreme = exhaustion/absorption ("hidden flip"). Close ≈95% of Max = full conviction. (Ch082/v256 [23:17]) `[v2][REPEATED across 15+ chunks]`
7. **ABSORPTION/ABANDONMENT** bonus — POC/VA not retraded next bar.
8. **CATALYST** — away from open/"unchanged".
9. **STOP feasibility** — small bar.

### `[v2]` 3.16 Back-to-back POC (new)
2+ consecutive bars share the exact POC price → magnet S/R; trade the **return** to it after price leaves (not while it sits there). Stop a few ticks beyond. (Ch082/v259) `[ONCE-rich]`

### `[v2]` 3.17 Order-flow Sequencing = CONTINUATION (anti-signal, new)
Escalating volume eaten *through* successive levels = continuation, **not** reversal. Use to **suppress** counter-trend fades. (Ch058,059,063,065,071,080) `[v2]`

### `[v2]` 3.18 Volume-in-value (Stratum) — orthogonal confirmation (new)
Volume distribution within the value area, **explicitly NOT delta-based** — a separate confirmation axis (don't mechanically stack). (Ch082/v257) `[ONCE]`

## 4. Common failure modes (unchanged + `[v2]`)
v1 list stands. `[v2]` add: **misreading close-delta** (ignoring intrabar Max/Min); **fading a flow-driven/sequencing day**; **over-weighting imbalance position-in-bar**; **holding past the ~1-hr order-flow freshness window**.

## 5. Guard-rails for the modeler `[v2]`
- "Not a red-light/green-light system"; confluence is a screen. (re-confirmed Ch082/v257)
- Thresholds **per-instrument AND per-timeframe** — `[v2]` exhaustion 10yr≈50/NQ≈3/ES≈0; neutral delta ±25 FX / ±150 heavy ES; follow-through off ≥5-min.
- `[v2]` **Time stop ≈ 1 hour / contextual** (not ~10 min); order-flow info freshness ~30–60 min, degrades beyond a 5-min chart. (Ch027,029,031,036,040,073)
- Single stop-out ≠ invalidation; second-chance re-entry. (unchanged)
- Order flow doesn't catch every turn. (unchanged)
