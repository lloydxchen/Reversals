## C. Order-flow story & psychology per setup
- **Exhaustion at the high:** aggressive buyers dwindle — first test of high traded 107 on the offer, second 19, third only 2; "do aggressive buyers really want to take this higher? No, because why is it only trading two contracts?" Buyers no longer interested → market can only go down (v480, [02:18]) [REPEATED]
- **Last-buyer concept (Tom DeMark anecdote):** a one-contract high tick = "the last buyer has bought… nobody else interested in buying." The single buyer was probing/testing to see if anyone would buy with them; nobody did → reversal (v491, [01:46]; v491, [02:12]) [ONCE]
- **Exhaustion at support (bullish):** aggressive sellers weaken into the level — delta −504, −139, −105, −53, −11; the aggressive selling "isn't present" anymore as price retests support → buyers can take over (v480, [03:59]) [REPEATED]
- **Absorption story:** buyers step in to support / sellers step in to offer resistance at a turning point; once price MOVES AWAY from that absorption you get "initiative trading coming in and that's where big trends can happen" (v484, [01:51]; v486, [01:18]) [REPEATED]
