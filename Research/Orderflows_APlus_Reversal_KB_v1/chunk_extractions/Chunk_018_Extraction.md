## C. Order-flow story & psychology per setup
- **Divergence/exhaustion at a high:** buyers keep lifting offers (delta still positive) but price makes equal/new high — the aggressive buying is no longer producing new price → move is "going to end... heavy volume often at the end of the move." (v1, [02:41]; v10, [11:44]) [REPEATED]
- **Capitulation at a low:** "everybody that's long was waiting for the market to come back... massive steep drop off, it stops, they think maybe I get a bounce and get out, then the selling comes in" — longs forced to puke. (v10, [23:36]) [REPEATED]
- **Delta surge = one side "piling in" / hedge funds going "risk off" all at once** — five hedge funds selling at once "shows up quite nicely" in delta. (v10, [02:40], [15:39]) [ONCE]
- **Value-area exit = new long-term money entering:** moving AWAY from yesterday's value area (not day-traders fading the low) = "more long-term money traders... new money, new positions coming in," seen in the imbalances. (v13, "Sept 1", [13:34], [14:01]) [ONCE]
- **Trapped on the breakout:** buyers who bought the breakout high get no follow-through; "we made a high but we didn't sell off... snapped right back" — failed-breakout indecision. (v2, [09:17]) [REPEATED]
