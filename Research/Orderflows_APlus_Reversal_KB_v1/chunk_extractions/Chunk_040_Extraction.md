## R. Contradictions / nuances

- **Negative delta at a low can be bullish**: explicitly contradicts the naive read — negative delta when buyers are working bids and sellers are selling into that support is a bullish sign, not bearish. This is a fundamental nuance for the delta divergence concept. (v114, [20:55]) [REPEATED — digest item]

- **Ratio fires on same or NEXT bar — both valid**: the ratio does not have to appear on the exact divergence bar; next-bar ratio is still an actionable entry. This slightly softens the "same-bar" strictness of some descriptions elsewhere. (v120, [04:03]) [REFINING]

- **Large negative delta in value area is not significant**: a −3,000–3,600 delta reading (which elsewhere would signal strong selling) is explicitly dismissed as unremarkable when price is in the middle of the value area, not at an extreme. (v116, [06:26]) [REFINING — magnitude without location = insufficient]

- **Three failed divergences = don't fade**: after three failed divergences, Mike explicitly says he would "watch out" and "wouldn't be surprised to see us break through that low" — reversing from a fade posture to a breakout posture. The threshold of three is stated explicitly. (v118, [05:43]) [REPEATED — digest item confirmed]

- **VWAP explicitly rejected**: unlike most retail frameworks, Mike explicitly does NOT use VWAP as a trade retracement target; states that modern VWAP trading has distorted the original institutional use, and that after 1 p.m. CT the VWAP flattens so much that retracements are not expected. (v114, [10:57]) [NEW ANTI-FILTER — VWAP as a retracement tool is explicitly ruled out]

- **Doji bars produce no ratio signal**: software deliberately excludes doji candles from ratio generation because Mike reads them as "kind of neutral" in order flow. This means a doji at a high or low even with large volume gets no ratio signal from his tool. (v114, [26:40]) [ONCE — implementation nuance]

- **Stacked imbalance with low individual volume = discounted**: a visually "stacked" pattern with volumes like 0v8, 0v10, 0v29 is "too light" for a "nice stacked imbalance." The stacked structure alone is insufficient; each component imbalance must also clear a meaningful volume threshold.
